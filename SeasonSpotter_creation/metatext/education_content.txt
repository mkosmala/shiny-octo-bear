Want to learn more about the PhenoCam Network?
----------------------------------------------
Visit the official [PhenoCam web site](http://phenocam.sr.unh.edu/webcam/about/)  to learn more about the scientific foundation and technical aspects of this remote sensing/plant phenology program.

Through a collaboration with NEON's [Project BudBurst](http://budburst.org/), we are in the process of creating [additional information and resources](http://budburst.org/phenocam) for use in education and outreach programs and activities.
